How It Started

Made with Heart, Served with Pride

740Eatz was born from a passion for creating treats that bring people together. What started as making sweets for family and friends in Portsmouth grew into something the whole community could enjoy.

Every tray, every strawberry, every coated piece of fruit is made fresh for your order — never sitting on a shelf, never made ahead. That’s the 740Eatz promise.

We’re proud to be part of the Portsmouth, Ohio community. When you order from us, you’re supporting a real family who genuinely loves what they do.

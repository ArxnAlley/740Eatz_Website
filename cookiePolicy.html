Cookie Policy

How 740Eatz uses cookies and how you can manage them.

Effective Date: June 30, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and understand how you interact with it.

Cookies do not contain personal information on their own. They are commonly used across the web to support basic functionality and improve the browsing experience.

Essential Cookies

This website may use essential cookies that are necessary for the site to function properly. These cookies do not track you for advertising purposes and cannot be opted out of while still using the site.

Essential cookies may be used for:

  • Maintaining basic website functionality across page loads.
  • Supporting form submissions and preventing duplicate requests.
  • Remembering temporary session preferences.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools such as Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the 740Eatz website. Analytics cookies collect information such as:

  • Which pages are visited and how long visitors spend on them.
  • How visitors arrived at the website (e.g., search engines, social media).
  • General geographic region (not precise location).
  • Browser and device type.

This data is collected in aggregate and anonymized. It is not used to identify individuals and is used only to improve the website experience.

Browser Controls

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • View and delete existing cookies stored on your device.
  • Block cookies from specific websites or all websites.
  • Set your browser to notify you when a cookie is being placed.

To manage cookies, check your browser’s help documentation. The exact steps vary by browser. Blocking certain cookies may affect how the website functions.

Cookie Management

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking specifically, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Opting out of analytics cookies does not affect your ability to use the website or submit an order request.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our practices change or as required by applicable law. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page.

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about ordering from 740Eatz in Portsmouth, Ohio.

Browse our menu, then click “Request an Order” on any product or in the navigation. Fill out the order request form with your name, contact info, product, flavor preference, and desired pickup day.

Once we receive your request, we’ll contact you to confirm the details before anything is made. No payment is required upfront.

We recommend placing your order at least 48 hours in advance to guarantee your preferred pickup day.

For large or specialty orders (large multi-flavor trays, event orders, etc.), 3–5 days notice is strongly preferred so we have enough time to prepare everything to our standard.

Candied Fruit: Bluerazz, Watermelon, Green Apple, Cotton Candy, Tutti Frutti. You can also order a large tray with three different flavors for $45.

Chocolate Covered Strawberries: Milk chocolate, white chocolate, or dark chocolate.

Cheesecake Stuffed Strawberries: Fresh strawberries filled with creamy cheesecake mixture.

Pickup is at 429 2nd St, Portsmouth, OH 45662. Exact pickup details and any additional instructions are confirmed when your order is finalized.

You may submit a request at any time. Orders are prepared for pickup on Monday, Tuesday, and Friday. Submitting early is recommended — we typically accept around 10–15 orders per pickup day.

If you need a different day, reach out through our contact form and we’ll do our best to accommodate.

Specialty orders — such as large event trays, combination orders, or requests with special themes — are handled through a personal conversation. Submit a request through the contact form or reach out on Facebook and we’ll work together to build the perfect order.

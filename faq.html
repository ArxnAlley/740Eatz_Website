Everything you need to know about ordering from 740Eatz in Portsmouth, Ohio.

Browse our menu, then click “Request an Order” on any product or in the navigation. Fill out the order request form with your name, contact info, product, flavor preference, and desired pickup day.

Once we receive your request, we’ll contact you to confirm the details before anything is made. No payment is required upfront — this is Version 1, which is a request-based system.

We recommend placing your order at least 48 hours in advance to guarantee your preferred pickup day.

For large or specialty orders (large multi-flavor trays, event orders, etc.), 3–5 days notice is strongly preferred so we have enough time to prepare everything to our standard.

Common Questions

Quick Answers

  • How do I place an order?

    Click “Request an Order” and fill out our simple form. Choose your product, flavor, size, and preferred pickup day. We’ll reach out to confirm before anything is made.

  • When can I pick up my order?

    Pickup is available Monday, Tuesday, and Friday at 429 2nd St, Portsmouth, OH. Look for the green awning. Submit your request anytime and we’ll confirm your pickup date.

  • Do I pay upfront?

    No upfront payment is required to submit a request. Payment details are handled when we confirm your order.

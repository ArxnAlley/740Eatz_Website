Privacy Policy

How 740Eatz collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective Date: June 30, 2026

Information We Collect

When you use the 740Eatz website, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — your name, phone number, and email address when you submit an order request or contact form.
  • Order details — product selections, flavor preferences, preferred pickup date, and any special notes you include in your request.
  • Technical data — your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited, collected automatically when you access the website.

We do not collect payment information through this website. No credit card numbers, bank details, or financial data are transmitted or stored here.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used only to operate and improve our services. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Review and respond to order requests.
  • Confirm pickup details and communicate with you about your order.
  • Respond to questions and messages submitted through the contact form.
  • Improve the website experience based on how visitors interact with the site.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

Cookies

This website may use cookies — small text files stored in your browser — to support basic site functionality. Cookies may be used to remember preferences and track general usage patterns.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the website. For more information, see our Cookie Policy.

Data Storage

Information submitted through our forms is transmitted securely and stored for the purpose of processing your request. We retain contact and order request information only as long as necessary to fulfill your order or respond to your inquiry.

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted to or from this website.

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services that may collect limited technical data:

  • Google Fonts — used to load typography. Google may collect basic request data when fonts are loaded.
  • Facebook — we link to our Facebook page. If you click those links, Facebook's own privacy policy applies.
  • Google Analytics — we may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the website. This data is anonymized and used only to improve the site experience.

We do not control the data practices of these third-party services. Please review their individual privacy policies for details.

Your Rights

You have the right to request information about what personal data we hold about you, and to request that we delete it. To make such a request, contact us directly using the information below.

We will respond to reasonable requests within a reasonable timeframe. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a deletion request.
